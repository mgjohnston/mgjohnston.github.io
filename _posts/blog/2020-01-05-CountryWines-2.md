Afer a roaring success, or rather learning experience of perrier and mead [link](inputme) , it is time for more homebrew.

### Pineapple Wine

* 3 small pineapples
* 1 kg sugar
* 250 g raisins
* yeast nutrient (boiled baker's yeast)
* Campden tablets
* Yeast EC-1118

### Apple Cider

* 4 litres apple juice
* 100 g raisins
* yeast nutrient (boiled baker's yeast)
* Campden tablets
* Yeast EC-1118

### Summer Fruits Wine

* 2 kg frozen summer fruits
* 500 g sugar
* 100 g raisins
* yeast nutrient (boiled baker's yeast)
* Campden tablets
* Yeast EC-1118
