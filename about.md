### Me
I'm a technology executive who ships production AI inside enterprise — player-coach, hands-on with the code, accountable for the business outcome. As CTO at Jollyes (TDR-backed, 100+ stores) I lead the engineering and data team end-to-end: payments, store systems, data platform, and most recently a [stateful MCP server](/building-a-stateful-retail-analyst-with-claude-and-mcp/) that lets non-technical colleagues query our retail warehouse conversationally through Claude.ai — replacing dashboards with grounded, business-aware answers. Before Jollyes I was Head of Marketing Data Science at Sainsbury's, leading the team that launched the AI recommendations engine cited in their quarterly results and tripled Argos' Meta ROAS.

I hire, mentor, and ship alongside engineers. I work across the stack — Python, TypeScript/React/Node, AWS, Airflow, Terraform — and care more about the customer outcome than the architecture diagram. PhD in Biology from the John Innes Centre ([open access papers here](../publications/)). Recently finished 10 years on the board of [UK Biology Competitions](https://ukbiologycompetitions.org/) (5 as Chair), running biology exams for 70,000+ students a year across 1,000+ schools. I'm actively looking for new volunteering opportunities for the public benefit, so if you need education, technology, retail, biology, or data experience please reach out.

Based London/Cambridge, six months' notice, open to conversations about applied or forward-deployed AI engineering leadership. Always happy to talk shop on retail, AI in production, or biology education.
