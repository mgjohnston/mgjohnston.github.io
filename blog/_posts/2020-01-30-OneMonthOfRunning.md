Day 30. A month ago I couldn't run; today I closed out January having run almost every day, mostly at 5k, sometimes farther.

The headline numbers from Strava:

- **30 days, ~148 km** of running (a marathon a week, give or take)
- 5k times nudged from ~35 minutes on Day 1 down into the 28s
- A sub-26-minute parkrun on Day 18 at Colney
- Two parkruns, one orienteering "5k" (which turned into 6k of confused jogging), and a lot of pre-work loops

What surprised me most isn't the running, it's the consistency. Going every day removes the daily decision of *whether* to run, and turns it into *when* and *how far*. The compression socks helped the shins, the knee brace helped the knee, and the stubbornness handled the rest.

Onto February.
